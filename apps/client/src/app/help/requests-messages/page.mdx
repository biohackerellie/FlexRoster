# Teacher Requests and Messages

<Callout type="warning">Only teachers have access to these pages.</Callout>

### Table of Contents

## Requests

<Callout type="info">
  <LineChart />
  The Request page can be accessed by clicking on the chart icon on the top bar.
</Callout>

![Requests](https://github.com/biohackerellie/images/blob/main/apps/flexroster/teacherRequest.png?raw=true)

The `Requests` tab shows all pending requests from students wanting to transfer to your room. The `Outgoing` tab shows all pending requests from students who are in your default Flex roster to another teacher.

### Approving or Denying Requests

<Callout type="info">
  To approve or deny a request, click on the flashing pending icon, then on
  either `approve` or `deny`.
  ![approval](https://github.com/biohackerellie/images/blob/main/apps/flexroster/teacher12.png?raw=true)
</Callout>

If you approve the request, the student will be automatically transferred to your class roster for the day but will still need to be marked as arrived. <Link href="/help/teacher-dashboard#marking-students-as-arrived" className="text-blue-500">`Click here`</Link> to learn how to mark students as arrived.
