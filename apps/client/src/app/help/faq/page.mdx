# FAQ

## What is FlexRoster?

FlexRoster is free and open source software, developed and maintained by [Ellie Kerns](https://github.com/biohackerellie). Designed to help with schools that have a flexible learning period to easily manage their schedules and keep track of students day to day as their schedules change.

## How does FlexRoster get my schedule?

FlexRoster syncs with your school's student rostering system every day to get the most up to date information.

## What rostering systems does FlexRoster support?

Currently, FlexRoster only supports Infinite Campus. If you would like to see support for other rostering systems, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/biohackerellie/flexroster).

## Can I take attendance in FlexRoster?

No, due to limitations by Infinite Campus, FlexRoster is unable to send attendance information back to them, only retrieve it.
